# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-07

## Local-first
The App primarily works by reading **local launcher manifests** and **local settings files** on your PC.

## What we read
When needed for features you use, the App may read:
- Steam/Epic manifest data and install paths
- Start Menu shortcuts
- Game config files (e.g., Unreal `GameUserSettings.ini`) when you apply per-game optimization
- VR settings files (SteamVR / Virtual Desktop / Vive) when you apply VR profiles
- Windows settings relevant to opt-in PC tweaks (registry/power/network) when you apply them

## What we store
Stored locally:
- Custom games you add (paths/args)
- App preferences
- Backups of files/settings before opt-in changes

## Network / telemetry
By default, the App does not require sending personal data to external servers and does not collect analytics unless explicitly added and disclosed in the UI.
