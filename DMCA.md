# DMCA / Copyright Policy — Unified Game Library

Last updated: 2026-05-07

This policy describes how to submit copyright or DMCA notices regarding distributions of this App or materials included with it.

## 1) Ownership
All trademarks, service marks, and product names (e.g., Steam, Epic, Xbox, Riot) are the property of their respective owners. The App is not affiliated with them.

## 2) What this App contains
The App is software that may read local launcher manifests and settings files to provide a unified library and opt-in optimization features. It is not intended to distribute copyrighted game content.

## 3) Reporting a copyright concern
If you believe that distributed copies of the App include content that infringes your copyright, send a notice to the distributor/maintainer of your copy of the App including:
- Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to be infringed
- Identification of the allegedly infringing material and where it is located
- Your contact information (name, address, email, phone)
- A statement that you have a good-faith belief the use is not authorized
- A statement, under penalty of perjury, that your notice is accurate and you are the copyright owner or authorized to act
- A physical or electronic signature

## 4) Counter-notice
If you believe material was removed or disabled by mistake, you may submit a counter-notice to the distributor/maintainer with the information required under applicable law.

## 5) Contact
Contact the distributor/maintainer of your copy of the App for the correct DMCA notice email/address.
